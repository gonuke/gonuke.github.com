How to find our house

(Maps & Directions)

3909 St. Clair St


View Larger Map

Coming from campus/downtown

By Car: Take University Ave west to Randall Ave and turn left (South). Veer right onto Monroe Street and continue for about 2 miles. Turn right at traffic lights on Glenway. Turn left at the top of the hill onto Glen and then immediately (literally) turn right onto St. Clair St. The house is at the end of the block on your left. NOTE: Glen and St. Clair Streets meet in an unusual intersection at Glenway.

By Bus: The #6 bus comes from State St, down Park St to Regent St, and along Speedway to Mineral Point Road. The other half stop at Toepfer on Mineral Point Road - it is a short walk down Toepfer. Other choices are the #7 bus on weekends, and the #3, #19, & #58, if you don't mind walking up the hill from Monroe st.

By Bike: The Southwest Bike Trail is marked in green on the map above. Turn right when you reach Glenway and follow the driving directions above.

Coming from the Beltline Highway

By Car: Take the Midvale Ave exit and go north on Midvale avenue. Continue for about a mile. Turn right on Tokay Blvd. After the park (on your left) turn left onto Toepfer. Turn right onto St. Clair (second right). The house is at the beginning of the block on your right.

Coming from the West/Middleton

By Car: Take Mineral Point Rd east past Midvale Ave. Turn right onto Toepfer (past Queen of Peace church/school and before traffic lights at Glenway). Turn left onto St. Clair (6th left). The house is at the beginning of the block on your right.

By Bus: The #6 bus comes from West Towne Mall via the West Transfer Point on Tokay Blvd. After the transfer point, it goes one on one of two routes. The "via Tokay" route will stop on Toepfer where the green dot is shown on the map. The other route will stop on Mineral Point Road at Toepfer and it is a short walk down Toepfer to St Clair. By Bike: Other than following the car directions above, the Southwest Bike Trail is shown in magenta on the map above. Ride the trail to Glenway, turn left and follow the car directions in the "From Campus/Downtown" section above.
