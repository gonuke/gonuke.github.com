The Gap of Dunloe

Co. Kerry

> Gap of Dunloe

The Gap on Dunloe was quite fascinating, even from the car. For others who might visit, I would strongly suggest walking or riding a bike (or horse) through the gap. In particular, if you approach from the North, arriving at Kate Kearney's Cottage, there are many options available here. One of the most popular options was to take a small horse-drawn buggy ride to the end of the Gap and walk back. Many others were riding bikes, walking, or riding horses.

The road winds through this narrow valley, climbing very slowly to the pass at the southern end. It is an easy walk with many waterfalls, steep faces, and green meadows.
