> Killarney

The drive from Killarney to Glengarrif on the N71 takes you through the breathtaking Killarnet National Park. This park epitomises the "green-ness" of Ireland and includes many ups and downs through the mountains of this region. This is also the first leg of the Ring of Kerry (assuming a clockwise start in Killarney). The rode wind around rocks, along lakes, up hills and through natural arches. Even on the foul grey and overcast day on which we experienced it, it was magnificent. The climax of the drive is Moll's Gap, which, even at only about 250 m, offers an amazing view of the valley which you have just traversed.
