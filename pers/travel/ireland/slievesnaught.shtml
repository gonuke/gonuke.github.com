Slieve Snaught

Co. Donegal

> Slieve Snaught

This walk really included three different peaks, Damph (~400m), Slieve Main (~500m) and Slieve Snaught (~600 m). We hiked them in this order and both valleys between were 100m down and 200m back up. The top of Slieve Snaught is covered with stones, many of which have been formed into Cairns by people over the ages. I like to believe that many of them were built by local youths who participated in a yearly pilgrimage to this peak as a social event until the beginning of this century.
