> Microfauna

In addition to all the birds, sheep and cows which populate some of the more remote
parts of Ireland, one comes across a wide variety of interesting looking insects, spiders,
snails and slugs. This brightly polka-dotted insect with big antennae was spotted
(no pun intended) on the Cliffs of Moher. (We'll have to get a proper lens for such
photos before our next trip!)
