The Doge's Palace

> Doge's Palace
In the middle of Venice, on Piazza San Marco, is the Doge's Palace. The Doge's of Venice were a long line of people chosen to rule Venice and surrounding areas. While it was not quite democracy, it also wasn't a hereditary system. To make our visit more worthwhile, we rented little audio-guides (you key in the number of each room as you arrive and it tells you about what you see).
