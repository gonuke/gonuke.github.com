The Doumo of Florence

The Duomo, here seen from across the River Arno, completely dominates the skyline of Florence. The Baptistry is also a large building, and in this picture would be just to the left.

These bas-relief doors on the Duomo are nearly as impressive as those of the Baptistry.

A close up of The Duomo shows the various patterns all made by intricate carving and the combination of various colours of marble.
