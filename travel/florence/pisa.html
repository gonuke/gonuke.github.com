Pisa: Tower and more

Believe it or not, people actually go to school here. In fact, the woman who guided us back to the right side of town after we got lost is studying in just such a building.

On the right here you can see that famous tower of Pisa. Although it almost seems upright here, remember that the cathedral does NOT lean!

Not quite as well known as the tower, the cathedral and baptistry which are in the same square are now used to generate revenue (you have to pay to get in) to stabilise the tower.
