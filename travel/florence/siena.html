Images of Siena

The Duomo in Siena is known for its "zebra" marbling. This cathedral sita atop one of the higher hills of Siena holding a prominent position.

In a closer look, it is possible to see the ornate carving of the stone which makes up the cathedral. The intricate fine carving seen here over the entrance is charateristic of the entire facade.

One of Siena's best known attractions is this Piazza del Campo. This view is from atop the tower which graces the town hall.

This tower atop the town hall is one of the highest points in Siena. Whether it is the cathedral on the other side of town, or simply the Piazza del Campo below, this tower offers an interesting view.
