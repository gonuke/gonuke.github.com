Hotel Wynberg in Zwolle, The Netherlands

Here are some (6) snaps of the Hotel Wynberg as it was at the beginning of November, 1996, in Zwolle in The Netherlands. You can click on each of the photos to get a bigger version (between 70 and 130 kB! each)! Each large photo is roughly 600x900 (150 dpi from 4x6 photo).
