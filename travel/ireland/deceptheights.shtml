Deceptive Heights

Sleive Snaught, Co Donegal

> Deceptive Heights

Here, at the summit (it is hardly a peak, let alone a summit) of Damph, you can nearly see the top of Sleive Main
to the left and can clearly see the top of Sleive Snaught to the right. The saddle betwen is roughly the same
altitude as the summit from where this photo was taken. One thing which we learned a lot about during our
hikes was the deceptiveness of heights. In this case, to us, Sleive Snaught did not look 200 m higher than we
were. It certainly didn't look as high as it felt once we climbed it!
