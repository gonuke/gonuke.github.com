Straight Down

The Cliffs of Moher, Co Clare
> Straight Down

This is a view over the edge of one of the cliffs along the Cliffs of Moher. As we arrived at this ledge,
just North of the nicely arranged official visitors area, there were two German's dropping things over the
edge and watching them fall. The last of these was a large rock which happened to be one of the
steps down to the ledge. To take this picture, I had to sit on Laurie's legs because she was afraid that
the weight of her head might cause her whole body to go over the edge.
