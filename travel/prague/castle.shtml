Prague Castle

> Castle
On the west side of the river, high above the rest of the city, is Prague Castle. The castle currently houses the presidential palace and hence the ceremonial guard. From the walls of the castle, you can get a great view of the entire city, either by day or night.

St. Vitus' Cathedral

The castle itself is "crowned" by the impressive St. Vitus' Cathedral.
