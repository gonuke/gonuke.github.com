Finding my Schedule on WiscCal

> WiscCal

To find my full schedule on WiscCal, you can follow these steps.

1. Log on to My UW

2. Click on the WiscCal button on the upper left:

This will open your default calendar in a new window.

3. Click on the "View Agendas" button:

This will open the "View Agendas" search window.

4. Enter my name "Paul Wilson" and click the Find button:

This will give basically the same page, but with my name in the list on the lower left.

5. Select my name from the list on the left and click "View":

This will open my calendar.

Note, as an alternative to 5. above, if you expect to look at my calendar freequently, you can add me to your favorites and return to your calendar. The drop down box at the top center will now contain my name.
